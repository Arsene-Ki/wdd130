The Republic Democratic of congo

The Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) is a cultural powerhouse, rich in traditions, music, dance, and art. Home to over 200 ethnic groups, it boasts a vibrant artistic scene, with Soukous music and Congolese rumba influencing African rhythms globally. Traditional dances, colorful fabrics, and storytelling remain central to its identity, reflecting its deep heritage and resilience.
